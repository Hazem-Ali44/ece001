What is Big Data?

Experts define big data as any group of data that is larger than the ability to process it using traditional data tools to capture, share, transfer, store, manage and analyze in a period of time that requires data; From the point of view of service areas, they are the tools and processes that organizations need to handle a large amount of data for the purpose of analysis. The two parties have agreed that they are formidable data that cannot be processed by traditional methods in light of these aforementioned restrictions.
